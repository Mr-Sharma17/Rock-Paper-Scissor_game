Rock Paper Scissor

About

Rock Paper Scissor is a hand-game, originated in China, usually played between two people, in which each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with an outstretched hand. These shapes are "ROCK" (a closed fist), "PAPER" (a flat hand), and "SCISSOR" (a fist with the index finger and middle finger extended, forming a V).

How to play?

You just have to click any one of the three buttons of your choice ("ROCK", "PAPER", "SCISSOR"), simultaneously computer will also made a choice. And as per the Rules result of the round declares.

Rules

  • Rock beats Scissors, but loses to Paper.
  • Scissor beats Paper, but loses to Rock.
  • Paper beats Rock, but loses to Scissor.
  • If both are same, round will be declared as DRAW.
  • For each WIN, you will be awarded 1 point, in case of DRAW, no points can be earned and if you LOSE, its GAME OVER!!
